Skill Movement — 6 Ways To Show It

Every option plots the same real payload: what the role expected (kitWeightedScore) vs what the candidate demonstrated (score), on a 0–10 scale. Flip between designs to compare. Current bars are the last tab.

Radar / spiderBest for a pitch

Two overlaid polygons — expected outline vs demonstrated fill. Shape reads as a verdict instantly. Weakness: needs axis filtering (drops kit 0), exact numbers hard to read past ~10 skills.

Dumbbell / connected dotsRecommended

One row per skill. Hollow dot = expected, filled dot = demonstrated, connector = the gap (red when short). Scales to all 20 skills, exact values readable, "not discussed" sits naturally at 0. Best signal-per-pixel.

Data notes (apply to all)

11 skills are "not discussed"

They have score 0. Row charts show them at 0 honestly; radar drops them. Keep the existing "N not discussed" collapse row either way.

4 skills demoed but not required

System Design, TypeScript, Socket.io, Redis: score 4, kit 0. No expected baseline — show as an "extra skills shown" chip line, not in the main chart.
